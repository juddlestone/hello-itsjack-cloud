001 — About

A bit about me

I currently work as an Azure Platform Engineer at Littlefish, a managed service provider based in Nottingham. Here I am aligned to one of the groups biggest clients, working within the customers platform team itself, providing thought leadership, guidance, and hands-on support to help mature their Azure estate, remove deployment friction, and deliver on their technical strategy.

I also contribute to Littlefish's internal platform initiatives, having built tools, solutions and automations that both improve our own business efficiencies and processes, whilst also giving engineers in the wider business access to reusable, customer agnostic tooling and scripts.

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my family and dog, being outdoors, gardening, and a good coffee and book. It goes without saying, that I also spend a lot of time learning and tinkering with technology.

003 — Way of working

How I like to work

I'm a big believer in keeping things simple. Clear documentation, small pull requests, and infrastructure that anyone on the team can understand — not just the person who wrote it. I'd rather automate something properly once than firefight it forever.

I like working in collaborative teams where everyone's comfortable asking questions and sharing knowledge, where the norm is challenged and new ideas are welcomed.
