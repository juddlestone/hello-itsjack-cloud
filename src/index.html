003 — Way of working

How I like to work

I'm a big believer in keeping things simple. Clear documentation, small pull requests, and infrastructure that anyone on the team can understand — not just the person who wrote it. I'd rather automate something properly once than firefight it forever.

I like working in small, collaborative teams where everyone's comfortable asking questions. I'm not precious about my code and I'd always rather someone challenged an approach than let something questionable slip through.

Leave it better Always improve what you touch, even if it's small.
Share the knowledge If only one person understands it, it's a risk not a solution.
Embrace change The best infrastructure is the kind nobody has to think about.
Automate the boring If you're doing it twice, it should be a pipeline.
